The Most Recent Game

TUBES 24 - 12 Old Colstonians

Man of the Tourny: Tom Stone (for consistently smashing the hell out of the opposition)

The day started well for me as I forgot my gum-shield, having to get my friend to deliver it to me. As Le Chaps were short on players both games were played 12 aside, 30 minutes each, which meant for some fast paced rugby!

The first game vs OCs started fairly evenly, with both teams having a few breaks. Tom eventually got some points on the sheet for TUBES after picking up a fumbled pass and running it straight under the posts through a disorganised OC defence. The second half went much better for TUBES, seeing Robin scoring two tries pacing it down the wing. Nick capitalised on their lack of fullback cover with a cheeky pizza ball (chip kick) which was picked up by Kirk who scored in the corner. Robin was denied his hat-trick after not quite managing to catch a tricky pass, unfortunately spilling the ball over the line. Despite the large size of the OC players the passing agility of the TUBES backs proved to be too much for them. A 28-12 victory!

Author: Alex Blake (Captain)
